Privacy Policy

Your data stays yours.

Spento is designed to be simple and private. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

Effective date: March 18, 2026

1. Who we are

Spento is provided by KUENG CREATIVE STUDIO. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at support@kuengcreative.studio .

2. What data Spento stores

Spento allows you to create and manage expense entries. Depending on how you use the app, this may include:

  • expense amount
  • category
  • note
  • date of the expense

3. How your data is stored

Spento stores your expense data locally on your device. At the current version of the app, your data is not uploaded to our servers.

4. Accounts and sign-in

Spento does not require you to create an account or sign in to use the app.

5. Analytics, tracking, and advertising

Spento does not use third-party analytics, does not track you across apps or websites, and does not display third-party ads in the current version of the app.

6. How your data is used

Your data is used only to provide the core functionality of the app, such as saving, displaying, editing, and deleting your expense entries.

7. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your expense data with third parties in the current version of the app.

8. Data retention

Your data remains on your device until you edit or delete it, or until the app is removed from your device together with its local data.

9. Children’s privacy

Spento is not directed specifically to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will post the updated version on this page and update the effective date above.
